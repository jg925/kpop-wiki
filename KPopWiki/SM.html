SM Entertainment

Founder: Lee Sooman
CEO: Kim Youngmin

SM Entertainment is one of the 'big 3' agencies in Korea. SM is known for its popular groups like SNSD, EXO, and Super Junior. Fans view this agency negatively at times because of the controversial scandals that happen. There have been many cases in which an artist will lawsuit the agency to leave. They argue about the unfair treatment and the inability to get adequate healthcare when needed. Despite this, SM Entertainment is still a popular agency among fans.
