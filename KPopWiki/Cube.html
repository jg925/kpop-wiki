Cube Entertainment

Co-founders: Shin Junghwa and Hong Seungsung
CEO: Shin Junghwa

Cube Entertainment is a growing agency. Although not as big as those in the 'Big 3', it is competing with JYP for a spot in the group. Cube Entertainment is known for its most famous groups, Beast and 4Minute. It is often mistaken as a subsidiary of JYP Entertainment due to Cube Entertinment's past relations.
